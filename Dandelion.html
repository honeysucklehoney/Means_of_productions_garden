Harvest

Harvest flower stalks after seed dispersal. Leaves can be taken when they're lush and green.


Uses

Pollinator friendly, weaver friendly. The stalks of dandelion can be used as a fibre. Leaves can be eaten raw or cooked. Flowers can be used to make dandelion honey.
