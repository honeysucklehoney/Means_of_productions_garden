Harvest

Harvest leaves in summer for dyes or skin salves.
Experiment in cordage and break apart a leaf. Pull out the inner fibres. Twist, twist, twist.


Uses

Leaves can be made into a skin salve, dye or experimental cordage.
Seeds can be collected as food. They make a good egg replacement in baking.
