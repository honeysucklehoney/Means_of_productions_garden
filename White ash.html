February, March, April, May:
Prune, mulch, weed and fertilize with compost tea. We could all use a warm cup of tea after so many rainy days.

June, July:
Weed. Bye Bye.

August, September, October:
Trim water shoots, weed some more, prune and serve compost tea.

November:
Snip, snip, snip. (Prune)
